Professional Banana Peeler.

Are you ready to go bananas? As a Professional Banana Peeler, you’ll be the Michelangelo of fruit, expertly peeling bananas with flair! Your mission: turn boring banana prep into a show-stopping spectacle. Get ready to impress at markets, food festivals, and even TikTok with your epic peeling skills!


Qualifications:
- Degree in Advanced Fruit Studies or a solid history of banana wrangling.


- Certified in Peelology (seriously, we need proof!).


Years of Experience:
- MAt least 2 years as a fruit peeler (bonus points for banana-only gigs!).


Skills: - Pro at various peeling techniques: The Classic Slip, The Tornado Peel, and more!


- Ability to peel while reciting the Kerala anthem (it's a challenge!).

Chief Coconut Water Tester

Are you ready to dive into the refreshing world of coconut water? As the Chief Coconut Water Tester, you’ll be the ultimate connoisseur of nature’s hydration! Your job involves tasting every coconut water you can find, from the classic to the weird (yes, there are weird ones). You’ll rate them on flavor, chill factor, and overall tropical vibes, ensuring only the best make it to the market!


Qualifications:
- Must have a degree in Coconutology or extensive experience in tropical beverage consumption.


- An Instagram account showcasing your coconut-tasting adventures is a must!


Years of Experience:
- At least 3 years in a beverage tasting role (coconut experience is a plus!)..


Skills: - Expert palate: Can differentiate between a fresh coconut and a “last week’s” one blindfolded!


- Ability to judge coconut water on creativity (e.g., best use of sparkles)..

Full-time Daydreamer

Do you excel at staring into space? As a Full-time Daydreamer, your job is to turn daydreaming into an art form! You'll drift into wild scenarios during meetings and conjure up epic adventures that may or may not involve unicorns. Perfect for those who think reality could use a little more magic!


Qualifications:
- Must have a wildly imaginative mind and a knack for creative zoning out.


- Certification in Advanced Daydreaming Techniques (self-taught preferred).


Years of Experience:
- At least 2 years of professional daydreaming.


Skills: - Can craft elaborate daydreams with unexpected plot twists.


- Expert at maintaining a serious face while lost in thought.
